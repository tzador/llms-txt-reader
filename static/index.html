llms-txt-reader.org

Repo | Issues | Discussions | HN Post | Discord

Renders to HTML remote llms.txt and the linked .md files, suitable for human reading.

Just prepend the URL of a llms.txt / llms-full.txt or *.md resource with https://llms-txt-reader.org/

Examples

  • https://llms-txt-reader.org/https://fastht.ml/docs/llms.txt
  • https://llms-txt-reader.org/https://docs.zenml.io/llms.txt
  • https://llms-txt-reader.org/https://www.better-auth.com/llms.txt
  • https://llms-txt-reader.org/https://svelte.dev/llms-full.txt
  • https://llms-txt-reader.org/https://llmstxt.org/llms.txt
